What Is Tech Together?

TechTogether is a non-profit founded for youth in the Lower Mainland to meet up and learn more about STEM!

At TechTogether code club, we learn how to code, build projects, games, websites, and learn skills such as CAD and more! We will also take part in HackClub's YSWS- You Ship We Ship. Head to the Contact page to learn more!

Inclusivity

Tech Together promotes learning and skills to everyone, regardless of past skills or other factors.

Learning Environment

We want to make STEM and technology fun, through activities, meetups, friendly competitions, hackathons, and more!

Growth

We aim to help youth grow- whether it be new coding languages, new skills, new ways of thinking, or new friends.

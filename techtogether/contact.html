More Info

As a club, we will be participating in HackClub's YSWS- You Ship, We Ship! At TechTogether, you will be guided into creating a project by experienced mentors. Then, the project is shipped to Hack Club in exchange for a variety of prizes, ranging from bubble tea to 3D Printers!

We will also be conducting weekly club meetings, where you will learn how to code, how to design in CAD, and other cool technology skills!

Founder and Leader

Hi! My name is Krupa Pramod, and I founded TechTogether in collaboration with Hack Club! I wanted to share my STEM skills with my community, so that we can make the field of STEM an inclusive environment for learning.

I’ll be leading the club and helping guide students as they learn and build new skills in a supportive environment. My experience across multiple coding languages, CAD, electronics, fabrication, and more will ensure positive growth amongst the club members.
